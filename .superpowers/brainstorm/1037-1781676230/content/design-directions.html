A — Warm Minimal

Airbnb meets Notion. Soft whites, warm accents, generous whitespace, rounded cards. Feels like a trusted travel companion. Clean and unintimidating.

Strengths

  • Most approachable for families
  • Feels premium and trustworthy
  • Easy on the eyes for long research sessions

Trade-offs

  • Light mode only (may need dark option later)
  • Could feel generic if not carefully branded

B — Modern Professional

Wise meets Linear. Cooler tones, indigo accent, subtle progress bars, light shadows. Feels data-confident without being intimidating. Agents would love presenting this.

Strengths

  • Balances warmth with credibility
  • Progress bars make data scannable
  • Works well for both families and agents

Trade-offs

  • Slightly cooler — less "cozy" than A
  • Indigo is common in SaaS — needs strong branding

C — Earthy Explorer

Kinfolk meets Google Maps. Sage greens, warm tans, grounded and organic. Feels connected to place and nature. Communicates "this is about your life, not just data."

Strengths

  • Most emotionally resonant for relocation
  • Unique — doesn't look like every other SaaS
  • Earthy tones pair naturally with map content

Trade-offs

  • Serif touches could feel less "techy"
  • Needs careful execution to avoid looking dated
